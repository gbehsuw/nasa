Sustainable Development Goals

What are we doing?

Sustainable Squad is a navigable website with information and activities, enabling high-school students to learn about the seventh Sustainable Development Goal (SDG), “Creating Renewable Energy for All on Earth”. The site features engaging and interactive text, images, and videos to teach the use of available sustainable energies in Canada. Additionally, there is an interactive activity in which students learn about an ongoing energy issue, and then create a DIY project to create their own renewable energy device.

What is our goal?

Our project is a self-guided website in which students can freely navigate and learn about sustainable energy, along with learning relevant issues and solutions presented by available data from NASA. There are multiple pages on the site where students can view the different types of sustainable energies, their applications in Canada, and other fun activities/resources to support their learning. Having an interactive website allows students to learn at their own pace. We hope that students, no matter their age, learn the potential of sustainable and renewable energy in Canada in a fun and interactive way through the website.
